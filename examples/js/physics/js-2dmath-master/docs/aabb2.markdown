## AABB2
  Stability: 1 (Only additions & fixes)

  BoundingBox2 is represented as a 5 coordinates array

  [left: Number, bottom: Number, right: Number, top: Number, normalized: Boolean]
* **TOPLEFT** = 1
* **TOPMIDDLE** = 2
* **TOPRIGHT** = 3
* **CENTERLEFT** = 4
* **CENTER** = 5
* **CENTERRIGHT** = 6
* **BOTTOMLEFT** = 7
* **BOTTOM** = 8
* **BOTTOMRIGHT** = 9
